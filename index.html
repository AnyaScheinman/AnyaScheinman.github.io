Ship and Planet game

I created this ship and planet game using C# and MonoGame. You use your mouse to control the ship and hit the planet. Once the ship hits the planet, you gain 10 points and the ship increases in size. The planet moves every 0.5 seconds to make the game more of a challenge. Added sound and animation.

Space Shooter

Using C# and MonoGame, I created a space shooter game. Every time you shoot an enemy with the laser, you gain 10 points. You can level up by earning 100 points. This has a moving parallax background as well as sound. The shit is keyboard controlled, and the laser is space-bar-fired.

Deer in Woods

I created this image using PhotoShop. This was for a final project that had to be about nature preservation. I added the word destruction to show how destroying our jungles and rainforests will also kill the wildlife that lives there. I have one side of the image with trees and a clear sky and the other side dead. To represent how logging has a massive impact on our ecosystem and how it can cause smoke and vast areas that are dead. Seeing the deer fading away on one side is stark and depressing. I wanted to have the deer partially gone on only one side to emphasize the contrast between life and death.
